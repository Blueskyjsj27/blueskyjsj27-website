Shapes

Shape: the external form, contours, or outline of someone or something. "she liked the shape of his nose"

In advertisements, Shapes and be used to put emphasis on important information, Seperating it out from the rest in a unique shape with a unique color

A shape is a great way to make certain information stand out and more eye catching, or even just fill in the background to make something appealing to look at.
