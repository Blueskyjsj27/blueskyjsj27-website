Lines in Visual Communication

line: a long, narrow mark or band. "a row of closely spaced dots will look like a continuous line"

In advertisements a line can be great for highlighting key phrases, seperate sections of a ad, make borders to draw attention, or use angled or curved lines to lead the eye toard a focal point

A line is great in visual communication, it can guide the eye, create structure, add depth and a lot more
