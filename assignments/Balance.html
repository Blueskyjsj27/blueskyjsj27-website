Balance

Balance: Balance in design refers to the distribution of visual weight within a composition. It ensures that no single part of the design overwhelms the other.


Radial Balance: Radial Balance occurs when elements are arranged around a central point, radiating outward in a circular or spiral pattern.


Symmetrical Balance: Symmetrical Balance, or formal balance, happens when elements on either side of a central axis are identical or very similar.


Asymmetrical Balance: Asymmetrical Balance is a more dynamic form of balance where elements of different visual weight or size are placed in a way that still achieves overall equilibrium.



Balance is needed in many things, balancing colors to make sure things mix well and not clash, balance can be found in advertising, music, web design and art. An example of Balance in music is making sure your instruments don't clash and you don't have too much clashing or sounds.
